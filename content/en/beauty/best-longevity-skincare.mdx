## What to look for in a longevity cream

Beyond the brand, the **ingredients with evidence** that justify the price:

### Tier 1 — Strong evidence
- **Retinoids** (retinol, retinaldehyde) — only ingredient with medical consensus for anti-aging
- **Stabilized vitamin C** (ascorbyl glucoside, MAP) — collagen + antioxidant protection
- **Niacinamide 5–10%** — skin barrier, hyperpigmentation, pores
- **Bioactive peptides** — Matrixyl, Argireline, OS-01

### Tier 2 — Emerging evidence
- **Growth factors** (EGF, TGF-β) — Augustinus Bader, SkinMedica
- **Bakuchiol** — more tolerable retinol alternative
- **Topical resveratrol** — antioxidant with sirtuin effects

### Tier 3 — More marketing than science
- Colloidal gold, platinum, caviar — premium effect, unclear mechanism
- Plant stem cells — most don't penetrate the skin barrier
- "Stem cells" without specification — be skeptical
