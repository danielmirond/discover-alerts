## What Is WHOOP?

WHOOP is a **performance wearable** focused on three pillars: training load (strain), recovery and sleep. Unlike other devices, WHOOP has no screen — all information is consumed through the app. It's a pure physiological data sensor.

**WHOOP 5.0** (launched late 2025) is the fifth generation, with significant improvements in sensors, size and battery life.

## Strain: The Killer Metric

The **Strain Score** (0–21) measures cumulative cardiovascular load throughout the day. Every activity — from a workout to a stressful meeting — adds strain. WHOOP auto-detects activities without you starting them.

The power move: WHOOP crosses your daily strain with your morning recovery to recommend optimal load. If your recovery is at 45%, it suggests an easy day. At 90%, you can push hard.

### How It Works

Based on:
- Continuous heart rate (24/7)
- Time in HR zones
- Duration and intensity of activities
- HRV and prior recovery state

## Recovery Score

Every morning, WHOOP calculates your **Recovery Score** (0–100%):

- **0–33% (red)**: your body needs rest. Avoid intense training.
- **34–66% (yellow)**: partial recovery. Moderate training is fine.
- **67–100% (green)**: full recovery. Push day.

Based on: overnight HRV, resting heart rate, respiratory rate, body temperature, SpO2 and sleep quality.

## Sleep Tracking

WHOOP tracks sleep automatically (no manual start needed):
- Stages: light, deep, REM, awake
- **Sleep Need**: hours needed based on strain, sleep debt and baseline
- **Sleep Performance**: % of need met (target: >85%)
- **Sleep Coach**: tells you what time to go to bed to hit peak performance tomorrow

## Who Is WHOOP For?

WHOOP is ideal for:
- **Athletes** training 4–6x/week who need load management
- **CrossFitters** and high-intensity athletes
- **Biohackers** who prioritise recovery and strain over daily smartwatch features
- People who want an **invisible** wearable (no screen, discreet)

NOT for you if:
- You want to see the time or notifications (get an Apple Watch)
- Sleep is your only priority (Oura Ring is better and cheaper)
- You don't want a recurring monthly subscription
