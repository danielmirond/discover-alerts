The mattress is the only biohack you spend **a third of your life** on. This is our comparison after testing the 4 main ones for 6+ months.

## Why a smart bed

Sleep science converges on three facts:

1. **Temperature**: your body needs to cool 1–2°C to enter deep sleep
2. **Regulation**: most bedrooms run 3–5°C above optimal
3. **Individuality**: couples usually prefer different temperatures (up to 5°C apart)

A smart bed solves all three simultaneously while giving you real sleep data without wearables.

## How to choose: decision tree

### 1. What's your budget?

- **Under $1,000**: BedJet V3 (forced air, no water, no maintenance)
- **$1,000–2,000**: Chilipad Dock Pro (real water-based thermal control)
- **$2,000+**: Eight Sleep Pod 4 (complete ecosystem)

### 2. Do you have a partner?

- **Yes, with different thermal preferences** → Eight Sleep or Chilipad Dock Pro (bilateral critical)
- **Solo or same temperature** → any works (BedJet single is enough)

### 3. Do you want sleep data?

- **Yes, without wearable** → only Eight Sleep detects sleep/HRV without extras
- **I already have Oura/WHOOP** → Chilipad or BedJet + your current wearable is more cost-effective

### 4. Does a subscription bother you?

- **Yes, I want to pay once** → Chilipad Dock Pro ($1,499 no fees)
- **I don't mind** → Eight Sleep (Autopilot AI justifies $25/mo)

## Factors most people ignore

### Noise
A noisy smart bed ruins the purpose. Real measurements:
- **Chilipad Dock Pro**: 28 dB (quietest)
- **Eight Sleep Pod 4**: 32–35 dB (acceptable)
- **BedJet V3**: 42 dB (noticeable, especially on high)

### Monthly electricity cost
- **Eight Sleep**: $10–18/month
- **Chilipad Dock Pro**: $6–12/month (more efficient)
- **BedJet**: $4–8/month (less power)

### Tank/motor size
All take space at the foot or side of the bed:
- **Eight Sleep**: 13×13×18 in
- **Chilipad Dock Pro**: 12×16×20 in
- **BedJet**: 12×16×12 in (most compact)
