## What the Eight Sleep Pod is

Eight Sleep isn't a mattress — it's a **smart thermal cover** placed over your existing mattress. It controls each side's temperature independently (50–110°F), detects your sleep stages without wearables and automatically adjusts temperature to your circadian cycle.

The **Pod 4** (2025) introduces significant improvements over Pod 3: pressure sensors 8x more accurate, bed that auto-elevates when snoring is detected, and a thermal engine 30% more energy-efficient.

## Why temperature matters

Sleep science is clear: **your body needs to cool 1–2°C to enter deep sleep**. In practice, most people sleep in rooms that are too warm, with sheets that trap heat. Result: high latency, fragmented sleep, less deep phase.

## How Autopilot works

The system has 3 automatic phases per night:

1. **Falling asleep phase (first 90 min)**: cool temp (60–66°F) to facilitate deep sleep entry
2. **Deep phase (90–240 min)**: maximum cooling (54–59°F) to maximize sleep architecture
3. **Wake phase (last 60 min)**: gradual warming to 72–79°F for low-cortisol waking

AI learns your pattern in 2–3 weeks and adjusts without you touching anything.

## The subscription — the controversial point

Eight Sleep requires **a $25/month subscription** to unlock:
- Automatic thermal Autopilot
- Advanced snore detection
- Sleep and HRV data in app
- Firmware updates

Without subscription, Pod works as manual thermal cover (control temp from app but no AI). Main complaint in negative reviews — you paid $2,995 and still have recurring cost.

## Installation and consumption

**Installation** (30 minutes):
- Spread the cover over your mattress
- Connect water tank at foot of bed
- Plug into power + WiFi
- App guides you through initial setup

**Electricity consumption**: $10–18/month depending on climate. More efficient than AC (you only cool the bed, not the room).

**Maintenance**: add distilled water every 2–3 months (indicator in app).

## Eight Sleep vs sleeping well for free?

Honest question: do you need Eight Sleep if you already optimize sleep with basic hygiene?

**You don't need Eight Sleep if**:
- You currently sleep well (Sleep Score >85 on Oura/WHOOP)
- Your room is already 60–66°F all night
- You don't sweat or have night sweats
- You don't have a partner with opposite thermal preferences

**You do need it if**:
- You struggle with deep sleep (less than 60 min per night)
- You and your partner need different temperatures
- You have menopause/andropause with night sweats
- You're a serious athlete and recovery optimization is critical
- High budget and you prioritize sleep quality above all else
